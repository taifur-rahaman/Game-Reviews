## Overview

Mafia: Definitive Edition is a faithful remake of the 2002 classic, rebuilt from the ground up with a new engine, updated script, and expanded story. Set in the fictional city of Lost Heaven during the 1930s, you play as Tommy Angelo, a cab driver who gets thrust into the world of organized crime. This remake aims to capture the original's cinematic atmosphere while modernizing the gameplay for a new generation.
