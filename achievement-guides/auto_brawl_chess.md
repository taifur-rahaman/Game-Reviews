## Overview

Auto Brawl Chess is an auto-battler that combines elements of auto chess, idle RPG, and CCG mechanics. The achievement list consists of 26 achievements that range from simple tutorial-style unlocks to extremely challenging leaderboard rankings.

**Difficulty Breakdown:**
- **Easy (1 achievement):** Can be unlocked immediately
- **Medium (10 achievements):** Require skill and strategy
- **Hard (11 achievements):** Hero-specific achievements requiring RNG and planning
- **Very Hard (4 achievements):** Leaderboard-based, extremely time-consuming and competitive

## Hero Star Level Achievements

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **These 16 achievements require reaching 3-star (maximum) with specific heroes in a RANKED match.** This is the most time-consuming part of 100% completion.

### Understanding What You Need to Do

For EACH of these 16 achievements:
1. Play a **Ranked Match** (not casual or dungeon)
2. Find and buy copies of the specific hero
3. Merge them until you have a **3-star version** of that hero
4. You do NOT need to win the match - just reach 3-star once
