## Overview

Auto Brawl Chess is an auto-battler that combines elements of auto chess, idle RPG, and CCG mechanics. The achievement list consists of 26 achievements that range from simple tutorial-style unlocks to extremely challenging leaderboard rankings.

**Achievement Categories:**
- **Easy (1):** Tutorial achievement
- **Hero Star Achievements (5):** Reach maximum (4-star) with specific heroes
- **Faction Achievements (11):** Win rounds with highest faction bonuses
- **Challenge Achievements (5):** Various gameplay challenges
- **Leaderboard Achievements (4):** Competitive rankings

**Step-by-Step Guide:**

1. **Queue for a Ranked Match**

2. **Understand what you need:**
   - You must collect **8 copies** of Marilyn
   - Merge them all the way to 4-star
   - This is very difficult in a single match!

3. **Strategy - Early Game (Rounds 1-10):**
   - Buy EVERY Marilyn you see immediately
   - Save gold but don't over-refresh yet
   - Build a functional team alongside Marilyn hunting
   - Goal: Have at least 2-4 copies by round 10

4. **Strategy - Mid Game (Rounds 11-20):**
   - You should have 2-star Marilyn by now
   - Start aggressive refreshing if you have gold
   - Keep buying every Marilyn
   - Other players might also want Marilyn - compete!
   - Goal: Have 3-star Marilyn (4 copies)

5. **Strategy - Late Game (Rounds 20+):**
   - You need 4 MORE copies to go from 3-star to 4-star
   - Refresh aggressively (spend all gold)
   - You need TWO 3-star Marilyns to merge into 4-star
   - This means: 4 copies â†’ 3-star #1, 4 more copies â†’ 3-star #2

6. **The Merge:**
   - When you have 8 total copies, you'll have two 3-star Marilyns
   - They automatically merge into **4-STAR MARILYN**
   - **Achievement unlocks!** ğŸ‰

**Pro Tips:**
- This is VERY hard to do in one match
- You need luck with shop rolls
- Consider focusing on Marilyn from round 1
- Low-cost heroes are easier (Marilyn might be higher cost)
- May take multiple attempts
