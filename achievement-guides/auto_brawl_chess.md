## Overview

Auto Brawl Chess is an auto-battler that combines elements of auto chess, idle RPG, and CCG mechanics. The achievement list consists of 26 achievements that range from simple tutorial-style unlocks to extremely challenging leaderboard rankings.

**Difficulty Breakdown:**
- **Easy (1 achievement):** Can be unlocked immediately
- **Medium (10 achievements):** Require skill and strategy
- **Hard (11 achievements):** Hero-specific achievements requiring RNG and planning
- **Very Hard (4 achievements):** Leaderboard-based, extremely time-consuming and competitive

## Hero Star Level Achievements

These 16 achievements require you to reach the maximum Star level with specific heroes in a **ranked match**. Maximum Star level is achieved by combining multiple copies of the same hero.

### Core Strategy for All Hero Achievements:
1. **Focus on one hero per match** - Don't try to max multiple heroes simultaneously
2. **Reroll strategically** - Use gold to refresh the shop when looking for your target hero
3. **Early game economy** - Save gold in early rounds to have more reroll potential
4. **Positioning matters** - Keep your target hero alive by placing them strategically
5. **Know the merge system** - Two 1-star heroes = one 2-star, two 2-stars = one 3-star

## General Tips & Strategies

### Understanding Synergies
The game features **10 Classes** and **11 Factions**:

**Classes:**
- Warrior
- Slayer
- Thief
- Guardian
- Soaring
- Warlock
- Shaman
- Shooter
- Priest
- Mage

**Factions:**
- Empire
- Drifter
- Tribe
- Plant
- Beast
- Reptile
- Dwarf
- Elemental
- Elf
- Undead
- Demon

**Synergy Tips:**
- Activate synergies by fielding multiple heroes from the same class or faction
- Synergies provide powerful bonuses (armor, healing, damage, etc.)
- Aim for 2-3 active synergies per team composition
- Some synergies are stronger than others - learn the meta

### Positioning Strategy
- **Frontline:** High HP, high defense warriors and guardians
- **Midline:** Balanced heroes with moderate survivability
- **Backline:** Ranged damage dealers, mages, priests, and support
- **Spread units** to minimize AoE damage
- **Protect key heroes** by surrounding them with tanks

### Gold Management
1. **Early Game:** Save gold for interest (if the game has an interest system)
2. **Mid Game:** Balance between rerolls and upgrades
3. **Late Game:** Spend aggressively to maximize team strength
4. **Board Expansion:** Expand your unit capacity when economically feasible
5. **Rerolling:** Only reroll when looking for specific heroes or upgrades

### Hero Upgrade Priority
1. **Common Heroes Early:** 3-star common heroes > 1-star rare heroes in early game
2. **Rare Heroes Late:** Transition to rare heroes in mid-to-late game
3. **Focus Upgrades:** Better to have one 3-star hero than three 1-star heroes
4. **Synergy Over Rarity:** A 2-star hero with synergies > 3-star hero without

### Match Strategy
1. **Scout Opponents:** Check enemy compositions to counter their strategies
2. **Adapt Positioning:** Reposition based on opponent threats
3. **Economy Balance:** Don't over-commit early; save resources for late game
4. **Win Streaks:** Maintain win streaks for bonus rewards (if applicable)
5. **Loss Streaks:** Sometimes strategic losing early can set up late-game dominance

### Achievement Hunting Tips
1. **Focus on Natural Achievements First:** Let easy achievements unlock through normal play
2. **One Hero at a Time:** When going for hero star achievements, focus on one per match
3. **Practice in Casual:** Learn strategies in unranked before attempting ranked achievements
4. **Study the Meta:** Watch top players, read guides, understand current strong compositions
5. **Be Patient:** Leaderboard achievements require significant time investment
6. **Track Progress:** Keep notes on which hero achievements you've completed

### Warlord Selection
- Each Warlord provides unique bonuses
- Match your Warlord to your intended strategy
- Experiment with different Warlords for different achievements
- Some Warlords may be better for specific hero compositions
