## üó∫Ô∏è Roadmap

### Step 1: Complete the Story on any Difficulty
Enjoy the narrative. Focus on gathering collectibles if you wish, but don't stress about it. You can replay chapters later. Use this run to get familiar with the driving and shooting mechanics.

### Step 2: Classic Difficulty Playthrough
Replay the game on **Classic Difficulty**. This is the hardest challenge. Enemies are lethal, police are strict, and driving is realistic.
*   **Tip**: The race in the mission "Fair Play" is the hardest part. Be patient, use the "ram" button to help cornering, and don't be afraid to restart.

### Step 3: Free Ride (Collectibles & Misc)
Load up "Free Ride" mode to complete the Phone Booth side missions, collect any missing magazines, cigarette cards, and hidden cars, and clean up miscellaneous combat achievements.

### ‚öîÔ∏è Difficulty Specific

#### üèÜ Made Man
**Description**: Complete the game on Classic difficulty.
**Strategy**:
*   **Driving**: Strict police (speeding tickets matter). Speed limiter is your friend (`L` key). Handling is "Simulation" style.
*   **Combat**: Enemies are aggressive and accurate. You die in a few shots. **Use Cover** extensively.
*   **Ammo**: Reloading discards all ammo remaining in the clip. Only reload when empty!
*   **The Race**: The hardest part. Feather the throttle in corners. Use the "Ram" mechanic (`F`) to tighten turns if you're taking them too wide.
