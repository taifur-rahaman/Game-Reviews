## üéµ Collectible Achievements

### üéº Music Box ‚ö†Ô∏è **MISSABLE**
**Description:** Listen to all the cylinders in the train  
**Difficulty:** 3/10  
**Missable:** YES - Must be done before finishing the game

**All 4 Music Cylinder Locations:**

1. **Cylinder 1 - "Forgotten Melody"** (Valadilene)
   - Location: Inside the train's music box (automatic during story)
   - When: Early in Valadilene

2. **Cylinder 2 - "A Terrible Accident"** (Valadilene - SECRET)
   - Location: Voralberg's Tomb in the cemetery
   - When: After opening Hans Voralberg's coffin
   - **CRITICAL:** This is easy to miss! Make sure to examine the tomb thoroughly

3. **Cylinder 3 - "War Rages On"** (Barrockstadt)
   - Location: Factory office in Barrockstadt
   - When: During the Barrockstadt section

4. **Cylinder 4 - "Epistolary"** (Komkolzgrad)
   - Location: Found during the Komkolzgrad section
   - When: In the cosmodrome area

**How to Listen:**
- Place each cylinder in the train's music box
- Listen to the entire melody for each one
- The music box is located in Kate's train compartment

## üí° General Tips

### Save Management
- **Use multiple save slots** (at least 5-6)
- Save before leaving each major location
- Save after talking to new characters
- Save after collecting important items
- Keep a "safety save" at the start of each chapter

### Dialogue Tips
- Exhaust all dialogue options with every character
- Some characters have multiple conversation trees
- Return to characters after story progression for new dialogue
- Don't skip any character interactions

### Item Collection
- Click on everything that highlights
- Check all interactive objects thoroughly
- Some items are hidden in plain sight
- Pick up items even if you don't need them immediately

### Music Cylinders
- Listen to each cylinder immediately after finding it
- The train music box is always accessible in Kate's compartment
- Don't wait until the end of the game to collect them all

### Puzzle Solving
- Take your time on first playthrough
- Make notes of codes and clues
- Some puzzles have multiple steps
- If stuck, try combining items or talking to characters
