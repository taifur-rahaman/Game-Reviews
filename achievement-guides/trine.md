## ‚öîÔ∏è Challenge & Skill-Based Achievements

### üéØ The Cool Way
**Description:** In a single level, kill one monster by jumping on it with the Knight, one with the Wizard's abilities, and one with the Thief's grappling hook kick  
**Difficulty:** 2/10  
**Best Level:** Academy Hallways  
**Tips:**
- Find 3 skeletons close together
- **Knight kill:** Jump on a skeleton's head with Pontius
- **Wizard kill:** Conjure a plank/box above a skeleton and drop it on them
- **Thief kill:** Grapple to a point above a skeleton and swing into them while holding the grapple button

### üóº What a View!
**Description:** Build a tower with at least 12 Wizard-created objects and stand on top without collapsing it  
**Difficulty:** 3/10  
**Best Level:** Academy Hallways  
**Tips:**
- Upgrade Amadeus's skill points to increase max active objects
- Create a wide base with planks/boxes
- Stack objects carefully - wider at bottom, narrower at top
- Stand on top with any character once stable

### üí• Whoops!
**Description:** Kill 3 monsters with a single physical object drop or throw  
**Difficulty:** 2/10  
**Best Level:** Academy Hallways  
**Tips:**
- Find 3 skeletons grouped closely together
- Conjure a large plank above them with Amadeus
- Drop the plank to crush all 3 at once

### üîÆ Still no fireball
**Description:** Conjure 200 objects in a single level  
**Difficulty:** 1/10  
**Best Level:** Any level  
**Tips:**
- Pick any level and repeatedly create planks or boxes with Amadeus
- You can create and delete objects repeatedly
- Takes about 5-10 minutes of spam-clicking

### ü¶¥ Spring master
**Description:** Complete at least 3 jumps in a row on different skeletons without touching the ground  
**Difficulty:** 3/10  
**Best Level:** Any level with multiple skeletons  
**Tips:**
- Use Pontius (Knight) for this
- Jump on a skeleton's head, then immediately jump to the next one
- Chain 3 jumps without touching the ground

### üëª Undead have rights, too!
**Description:** Complete a level other than Astral Academy without directly killing any enemies  
**Difficulty:** 2/10  
**Best Level:** Academy Hallways  
**Tips:**
- Avoid all enemies - run past them or use platforms to go over them
- You can let enemies fall to their death (environmental kills don't count)
- Use Thief's grappling hook to bypass combat areas

### üèä Summer Dip
**Description:** Stay underwater for more than 20 seconds without taking damage  
**Difficulty:** 2/10  
**Best Level:** Crystal Caverns  
**Tips:**
- Find a safe underwater area with no enemies
- Rotate between all 3 characters as their breath meters get low
- Each character has their own oxygen meter

### üéñÔ∏è Better Than Developers!
**Description:** Complete Tower of Sarek without any deaths on hardest difficulty  
**Difficulty:** 6/10  
**Requirements:** Hard difficulty (not Hardcore mode required)  
**Tips:**
- Play very carefully and methodically
- Use Thief's bow to kill enemies from a distance
- Take your time - there's no rush
- If a character dies, restart the checkpoint immediately

### üö∂ Walk in the Park
**Description:** Complete the game on hard using hardcore mode  
**Difficulty:** 7/10  
**Requirements:** Hard difficulty + Hardcore mode enabled  
**Tips:**
- **Hardcore mode:** Characters can't be revived until next checkpoint
- If all 3 die, you restart the entire level
- Play extremely cautiously
- Eliminate all enemies before proceeding
- Use ranged attacks with Thief whenever possible
- If a character dies, restart checkpoint to avoid it counting

- Very easy to get naturally

### üõ°Ô∏è Survivalist

**Difficulty:** 4/10

**Tips:**
