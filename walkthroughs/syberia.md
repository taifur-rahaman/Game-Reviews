## Overview

**Game:** Syberia (2002)  
**Genre:** Point-and-Click Adventure  
**Estimated Completion Time:** 10-15 hours  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Missable Content:** None (Linear story)

This guide provides **extreme detail**, covering every item pickup, conversation, and puzzle solution required to finish the game.

---

## Getting Started

### Important Controls
- **Left Click:** Move / Interact / Pick up
- **Right Click:** Open Inventory / Back out of view
- **Double Click:** Run
- **Esc:** Menu

### Tips
- **Talk to Everyone:** Information triggers progress. Exhaust all "Mission" and "Help" options.
- **Documents:** Read everything you pick up; they contain clues and phone numbers.
- **Phone:** Your cell phone is in your inventory. You can call numbers found in documents.
- **Save Often:** There is no autosave.

## Chapter 1: Valadil√®ne

### The Arrival & Hotel
1.  **Lobby:**
    *   **Entrance:** After the opening cutscene, you arrive at the hotel. Observe the notice board immediately to your left. Pick up the **Advertising Brochure**.
    *   **Momo:** You will see a boy (Momo) sitting at a table working on gears. Attempt to pick up the **Cogs** on the table and floor. Momo will growl/object, preventing you from taking them for now.
    *   **The Counter:** Walk to the reception desk at the back. Try to talk to the man; he is busy.
    *   **Bell Puzzle:** Pick up the **Reception Bell Key** lying on the counter. Use the **Key** on the small bell automaton holding a hammer. Press the **Red Button** to ring the bell.
    *   **Check-in:** The manager appears. Talk to him. Select "Help" to have him take your suitcase. He leads you to your room.

2.  **Room #6:**
    *   **Fax:** Once in the room, go to the nightstand. Open the drawer and pick up **Fax #1** (from Marson & Lormont).
    *   **Phone Call:** Open your inventory (Right Click), select the **Cell Phone**. Dial the office (`12-458-902`) or simply select "Office" from the phone book. Talk to your boss.
    *   **Leaving:** Exit the room and go downstairs.

3.  **Lobby (Part 2):**
    *   **Introduction Letter:** Talk to the Hotel Manager again. Select "Mission". He will verify your identity and give you **Fax #2** (Letter of Introduction from the Notary).
    *   **Collecting Gears:** Now that Momo has left, go to his table.
        *   Pick up **2 Cogs** from the floor.
        *   Pick up **2 Cogs** from the table.
        *   (You should now have 4 Cogs total).
    *   **Exit:** Leave the hotel.

### The Notary
4.  **Village Street:**
    *   **Baker:** Walk left from the hotel. Pass the bakery (you can interact with the door/window, but it's closed).
    *   **Notary's House:** Continue to the house with the metal automaton gatekeeper.
    *   **Newspaper:** Before interacting with the door, check the bench nearby. Pick up the **Gazette** (Valadil√®ne Gazette). It announces Anna Voralberg's death.

5.  **Entry Puzzle:**
    *   Zoom in on the automaton at the door.
    *   **Step 1:** Place **Fax #2** (Letter of Introduction) into the automaton's hand.
    *   **Step 2:** Pull the **Middle Lever** (on its chest).
    *   **Step 3:** Pull the **Right Lever** (on its side).
    *   The door opens. Enter the hallway.

6.  **Meeting the Notary:**
    *   Go through the door on the left (Notary's office).
    *   Walk to the desk and sit in the chair.
    *   **Dialogue:** Talk to the Notary.
        *   "Mission": Explanation of the sale.
        *   "Anna": Discussion about her death.
        *   "Hans": The revelation that Hans is alive and is the heir.
    *   **Items:** He gives you **Anna's Letter** (confession).
    *   **Leaving:** Exit the office to the hallway. Look at the coat rack/umbrella stand near the door. Pick up the **Telescopic Key**. Exit the building.

### The Voralberg Factory
7.  **Factory Gate:**
    *   Walk right, past the hotel, to the far end of the village.
    *   Approach the large metal gates.
    *   **Gate Puzzle:**
        *   Use the **Telescopic Key** on the upper lock (held by an automaton).
        *   Turn the key (wind the lower mechanism).
        *   Pull the lever at the bottom.
        *   The gate opens.

8.  **Factory Grounds:**
    *   **Fountain Paths:** You are at a central fountain.
        *   **Left Path:** Leads to the Metal Press factory.
        *   **Right Path:** Leads to the Voralberg House and Train Station.
        *   **Top Right:** Leads to the private garden.
    *   **Go Right:** Head towards the house entrance. Walk *around* the house to the back.
    *   **Gardens:** Continue past the back of the house to the maze-like garden.
    *   **Secret Area:** Find the small fountain basin at the end of the maze.
        *   Zoom in on the basin.
        *   Use **Telescopic Key** on the automaton holding the basin.
        *   The basin opens. Pick up the **Voralberg Key**.

9.  **The Attic (House):**
    *   Return to the back of the house. Locate the ladder that looks like a metallic spine.
    *   Use the **Voralberg Key** on the ladder mechanism. The ladder descends. Climb up.
    *   **Desk:** Go to the large desk in the corner.
        *   Open the lid. Pick up the **Ink Bottle**.
        *   Pick up **Anna's Diary**. Read it for backstory.
    *   **Momo Encounters:** Walk to the dark corner on the right.
        *   Click the **Lightbulb** overhead to turn it on. Momo appears (scaring you).
        *   Talk to Momo: "Mission", "Hans", "Mammoth".
        *   He gives you **Pencil and Paper**.
    *   **Mammoth Tracing:**
        *   Look closely at the wooden beam near the lightbulb. There is a faint etching of a mammoth.
        *   Use the **Pencil and Paper** on the etching.
        *   Receive **Mammoth Tracing**.
    *   **Follow Momo:** Give the **Mammoth Tracing** to Momo. He gets excited and promises to show you a secret. Follow him out of the window/attic.

### The Secret Cave & Dam
10. **The Chase:**
    *   Follow Momo back through the factory gate, turn left, and go toward the park/stream area (past the town gate).
    *   Enter the park gate. Follow Momo up the stairs to the dam.

11. **The Dam Puzzle:**
    *   Momo is sitting on a rock. Talk to him. He wants you to open the dam so the water drops.
    *   **Attempt 1:** Try to turn the dam wheel. It's stuck.
    *   **The Oar:** Walk past Momo towards the boat. Try to pick up the **Oar**. It's floating too far out.
    *   **The Lever:** Look at the ground near the dam. Pick up the **Broken Lever** (a stick).
    *   **Retrieving Oar:** Go back to the boat. Use the **Broken Lever** on the **Oar** to pull it closer. Pick up the **Oar**.
    *   **Asking for Help:** Return to Momo.
        *   Use the **Oar** on Momo? No, just ask him for help (Dialogue: "Help"). He is strong.
        *   Kate uses the Oar as a lever, Momo pushes. The dam opens.

12. **The Cave:**
    *   Go back down the stairs to the river bed (now dry/crossable).
    *   Cross the river and enter the cave.
    *   Walk to the very back. Pick up the **Mammoth Doll** lying on the ground.
    *   Return to the Factory fountain.

### Factory Operations
13. **The Workshop:**
    *   Take the **Left Path** from the fountain into the main factory building.
    *   **The Forklift:** Immediately inside, toggle the switch on the forklift automaton. It drives forward, carrying a metal cylinder to the assembly line.
    *   **The Engineer:** Enter the door behind where the forklift was.
        *   You see a large automaton hanging from the ceiling.
        *   Pull the winch chain. It lowers partly but stops. You need to finish the assembly.
        *   Exit back to the main floor.

14. **Power Room:**
    *   Go to the far back right of the main factory floor (follow the tracks/machinery).
    *   Enter the room with the water wheel mechanism.
    *   **Activate Power:** Walk behind the mechanism. Pull the **Lever/Chain**.
    *   The gears engage with the water wheel outside. The factory now has power.

15. **Anna's Office:**
    *   Go up the metal stairs near the entrance/forklift to the office on the catwalk.
    *   **Secret Cabinet:** Look at the bookshelf. One book has a white spine. Click it/Pull it.
    *   The cabinet rotates. Pick up the **Music Box Cylinder**.
    *   **Blueprints:** Look at the desk. Pick up:
        *   **Hans-Anna Toy** (statue).
        *   **Factory Blueprint**.
        *   **Train Blueprint**.
        *   **Voice Cylinder** (Valadil√®ne).
        *   **Letter to Hans**.

16. **Production Line (Making Legs):**
    *   Go back down to the main floor. locate the **Control Panel** near the conveyor belt (middle of room).
    *   **Setup:**
        *   Look at the empty slot on the panel. Insert the **Factory Blueprint**.
        *   This activates the controls.
    *   **Configuration:**
        *   **Left Switch (Material):** Turn to the far left position (reddish color icon? or default wood). It needs to be **Light Wood**. (Usually just ensuring it's on the correct setting by checking the color match with the legs).
        *   **Right Switch (Part):** Turn the wheel until the indicator lights up the **Legs** (bottom icon).
    *   **Execution:** Press the designated **Production Button** (right side).
    *   **Result:** The machine starts. Watch the process.
    *   **Collection:** Walk to the end of the conveyor belt (near the hanging engineer). Pick up **Oscar's Legs**.

### Oscar & The Train
17. **Completing Oscar:**
    *   Go into the small workshop room again.
    *   Use **Oscar's Legs** on the hanging automaton.
    *   He wakes up! His name is Oscar.
    *   Talk to him. He leaves to take his post at the station.

18. **The Station:**
    *   Leave the factory building. Go to the Train Station (Right path from fountain).
    *   **Ticket Booth:** Oscar is inside the booth.
        *   Talk to him ("Ticket").
        *   He gives you a **Train Ticket** and a **Train Release Permit**.
    *   **The Train:** Enter the train. Try to give him the ticket. He says everything must be in order.

19. **The Permit (Legal):**
    *   The permit needs an official stamp.
    *   Run back to the village **Notary**.
    *   Enter the reception. (The Notary is gone, but the office is open).
    *   Look at the stamping machine on the reception desk (the little hat man).
    *   **Stamp Puzzle:**
        *   Open the hat lid.
        *   Pour **Ink Bottle** into the hat.
        *   Place **Train Release Permit** on the flat pad.
        *   Press the **Red Button**.
        *   Retrieve **Stamped Train Release Permit**.
    *   Return to the Station.

20. **The Archives (Items):**
    *   Board the train. Talk to Oscar again.
    *   He mentions the "Archives" are empty (Room in the train).
    *   Go to the center room of the train carriage (Archive Room).
    *   **Place Items:**
        *   Place **Hans-Anna Toy** on the central pedestal.
        *   Place **Mammoth Doll** on the bottom right shelf.
        *   Place **Music Cylinder** on the shelf.
        *   Place **Voice Cylinder** on the shelf.
        *   Talk to Oscar again. He is satisfied with the archives.

21. **Winding the Train:**
    *   Give Oscar the **Stamped Train Release Permit**.
    *   He accepts it but says the train isn't wound.
    *   **The Mechanism:**
        *   Exit the train.
        *   Walk across the tracks to the far platform (there's a crossing).
        *   Walk down the platform to the **Winding Machine** (Wheel and Lever).
        *   **Action:** Turn the **Wheel** (extends key). Pull the **Lever** (winds spring). The key retracts.
    *   (Optional but recommended: Talk to the man in the garden nearby/Mausoleum for lore, but strictly for walkthrough, just wind it).

22. **Departure:**
    *   Board the train.
    *   Give Oscar the **Train Ticket**.
    *   Sit back and watch the cutscene as the train leaves Valadil√®ne.

## Chapter 2: Barrockstadt

### The Station & University
1.  **Arrival:**
    *   **The Walk:** Exit the train. Walk to the right (towards the rear of the train) along the platform. Pick up the **Hook** lying on the ground.
    *   **Station Master:** Walk all the way to the front left of the platform. Exit the station building to the canal side. Speak to the **Station Master**. Ask about "Birds" and the "Hook".

2.  **The University:**
    *   **Entrance:** From the train platform, exit to the right side (the grand building). Enter the main hall.
    *   **The Rectors:** Go to the large central double doors. Enter the **Rectors' Office**. Talk to the three men.
        *   "Money": They agree to pay you if you fix the bandstand.
        *   "Sauvignon": They claim no knowledge.
    *   **The Library:** Exit the office. Go to the second floor (stairs in the main hall). Enter the Library.
        *   **Book:** Climb the ladder on the shelf. Pick up the **blue book** (upside down). It is about the **Amerzone Cuckoo**. Read it: it mentions they love Sauvignon grapes.
    *   **Professor Pons:** Go to the other wing of the main hall (Skeleton Hall).
        *   Talk to **Professor Pons** on the ladder. Talk about "Hans", "Mammoth".
        *   He asks for the **Mammoth Doll**. Give it to him.
        *   He invites you to his lecture (Laboratory).
    *   **The Laboratory:** Follow Pons to the lab (side room).
        *   **Items:** Pick up the **Barrockstadt Voice Cylinder** and the **Yangala-Cola Powder** from the shelves/tables.
        *   Pick up the **Test Tube Holder** from the lab bench.

### The Mystery of the Grapes
3.  **The Station Master's Secret:**
    *   Return to the **Station Master** (canal side). Ask about "Sauvignon". He is evasive and runs away.
    *   Follow him back into the station building (over the bridge).
    *   Ask him again about "Sauvignon". He admits there is a secret garden. He unlocks the gate for you.

4.  **The Garden:**
    *   Go to the far end of the platform (left side), cross the bridge over the tracks.
    *   Enter the garden gate.
    *   Walk to the very end of the jungle path.
    *   Pick up the **Sauvignon Grapes**.

5.  **The Cuckoos:**
    *   Return to the train platform.
    *   Nearby the train, there is a ladder going up to the mechanical eagle. The ladder is blocked by birds.
    *   **Puzzle:** Give the **Sauvignon Grapes** to the birds. They fly away.
    *   Climb the ladder.
    *   **Eagle Egg:** Use the **Test Tube Holder** on the eagle nest/mechanism. You scoop out the **Cuckoo Egg**.

### Fixing the Bandstand
6.  **The Bandstand:**
    *   Exit to the University courtyard (outside the main entrance).
    *   Approach the Bandstand (Gazebo).
    *   Zoom in on the door mechanism.
    *   **Balance:** Use the **Cuckoo Egg** on the empty scale pan.
    *   Turn the wheel. The gate opens.
    *   Enter the bandstand. Pull the lever inside to start the automaton band.

7.  **Payment:**
    *   Return to the **Rectors**.
    *   Talk about "Money". They give you **100 Dollars**.

### The Locks
8.  **The Bargemen:**
    *   Go to the canal. Talk to the couple on the barge. They want $100 for passage.
    *   Give them **100 Dollars**.
    *   They give you the **Lock Key** (by throwing it on the concrete; pick it up).

9.  **The Control Panel:**
    *   Walk down the canal path to the Lock Control Panel.
    *   **The Code:** Look at the sign: "Help: 27-66-67-42". Use your **Cell Phone** to call this number.
    *   **Instructions:** The technician says:
        *   Press #4 to lower water/open locks.
        *   Press #2 to open sluice.
        *   Press #1 to close.
    *   **Execution:**
        *   Use **Lock Key** to open the panel cover.
        *   **Step 1 (Lower Water):** Enter **4**, **2**, **Star (*)**. The water lowers, and the barge enters the lock.
        *   **Step 2 (Raise Water):** Once the barge is inside, enter **4**, **1**, **Star (*)**. The water rises, and the barge exits the lock.
    *   Watch the barge move.

### Departure
10. **The Wall & Captain:**
    *   Walk to the far end of the platform (towards the exit wall).
    *   Talk to **Captain Ombu** on the wall. He can't see the "enemy".
    *   **Telescope:** Look through the telescope. Adjust the focus (**Right Button** 3 times? Just use the buttons until clear) until you see the Cossack rider clearly.
    *   Talk to the Captain again. "Cossack". He realizes it's fake.
    *   He grants permission to pass.

11. **Winding the Train:**
    *   Board the train. Speak to Oscar. He says the train isn't wound.
    *   Exit the train. You are now near the winding mechanism (the barge pulled the train here).
    *   Wind the train (Turn Wheel, Pull Lever).
    *   Re-board. Depart for Komkolzgrad.

## Chapter 3: Komkolzgrad

### The Industrial Complex
1.  **Arrival:**
    *   **The Problem:** The train stops. Oscar mentions hands are "jammed".
    *   **The Giant:** Exit the train. Walk past the train to the giant automaton worker.
    *   **Climbing:** Look at the foot of the giant. Climb the ladder into the cockpit.

2.  **The Giant Cockpit:**
    *   **Items:** Look at the shelf inside. Pick up the **Komkolzgrad Voice Cylinder**.
    *   Pick up the **Handle** (Control Lever).
    *   **Controls:** Insert the **Handle** into the slot.
    *   **Action:** Push the handle **UP** (Forward) **twice** to align the giant with the train.
    *   **Winding:** Press the **Red Button** on the console. The giant winds the train.
    *   **Return:** Pull the handle **DOWN** (Backward). The giant retracts.
    *   Exit the cockpit.

3.  **The Theft:**
    *   As you climb down, you see a man running away (Serguei).
    *   Run back to the train carriage.
    *   Enter the train. Go to the engine room. Oscar is tied up. His **Hands** are stolen!
    *   **Key Item:** Return to the Giant Cockpit.
    *   Look at the small metal shelf inside the cockpit.
    *   Pick up the **Metal Shears** (Wire Cutters).

### The Infiltration
4.  **Entering the Factory:**
    *   Walk to the metal wall alongside the train. There is a boarded up/metal plated window.
    *   Use the **Metal Shears** on the metal plate to cut a hole.
    *   Enter the factory.

5.  **The Organist:**
    *   You are in a large hall with a massive organ.
    *   **The Automaton:** Pick up the **Screwdriver** sticking out of the generator/automaton near the organist (Boris).
    *   **Boris:** Talk to the Organist (Boris Charov). He is unresponsive/drunk.

6.  **Serguei's Room:**
    *   Go to the smelting area (control room).
    *   Serguei locks the door. He refuses to give the hands back unless you bring Helena Romanski.

### The Museum & Helena
7.  **The Museum:**
    *   Go to the elevator at the back of the factory (near the organ).
    *   Enter the museum.
    *   **Items:** Open the drawer in the display cabinet. Pick up the **Scrapbook**.
    *   **Letters:** Read the letters.

8.  **Sobering Boris:**
    *   Go back to the main hall near the organ.
    *   Go down the other path to the centrifuge/space capsule room.
    *   Boris is passed out near the capsule.
    *   **Mechanism:** Go to the overhead crane control panel (climb the ladder).
    *   **Key:** Pick up the **Key** lying on the console. Insert it.
    *   **Crane Puzzle:**
        *   Move the crane **Left** and **Down** (position over Boris).
        *   Turn the **Valve/Switch** (Shower icon) to spray him with water. He wakes up.

9.  **The Airship Key:**
    *   Go down and talk to Boris.
    *   Ask about "Bird", "Airship". He says the airship works but he threw away the key into the "Preserve".

### The Airship Departure Prep
10. **Automaton Bird:**
    *   Boris enters the capsule (Space Cockpit). He asks you to launch him.
    *   **Launch Sequence:** Insert **Key** (his key). Press buttons. He launches.
    *   **Item:** He drops a **Crank Handle** on the floor. Pick it up.

11. **Retrieving the Airship Key:**
    *   Go to the **Bird Cage** (in the Organ room).
    *   Use the **Crank Handle** on the cage. The mechanical eagle flies out.
    *   Go to the factory lobby (where you entered with shears). There is a large locked door nearby? No, go back out the hole?
    *   Actually, just go to where the eagle went. It hovers over the rubble in the outdoor area (the Preserve entrance).
    *   Pick up the **Airship Key** from the ground/rubble near the red water.

### Escape
12. **Dynamite:**
    *   Before leaving, check the closet/boxes in the space control room where you woke Boris.
    *   Pick up the **Dynamite**.
    *   Return to the Train (outside).
    *   **Giant:** Walk to the Giant Automaton's leg.
    *   Place **Dynamite** on the leg.
    *   Boom. It falls over, creating a bridge.

13. **Departure:**
    *   Climb the Giant/Bridge to the upper landing.
    *   Walk to the Airship.
    *   Use the **Airship Key** to unlock it.
    *   Pull the lever. You fly to Aralbad.

## Chapter 4: Aralbad

### The Resort
1.  **Arrival:**
    *   Kate arrives at the windswept Aralbad spa.
    *   **Front Desk:** Enter the hotel. Talk to the manager Felix. He won't let you see Helena.
    *   **Utility Room:** Look at the small door to the left of the desk. Pick up **Detergent**.
    *   **The Fountain:** Go outside. Pour **Detergent** into the fountain.
    *   Go back inside. Ring the desk bell. The manager rushes out to clean the suds.
2.  **Infiltration:**
    *   Go behind the desk.
    *   Look at the **Guest Register**. Note Helena's code: **1270**.
    *   Pick up the **Brochure** (Blue Helena recipe).
    *   Press the **Red Button** under the desk. The iron gate to the spa opens.

### The Spa & Helena
3.  **The Pools:**
    *   Enter the pool hall. The way is blocked by the robot James.
    *   **Keypad:** Go to the keypad. Enter **1270**.
    *   **Gas Mask:** Enter the locker room. Pick up the **Gas Mask** hanging on a hook.
    *   **Access Card:** Look for a **Temporary Access Card** in the locker room (on the floor/bench). Note the code on it: **0968**.
    *   **Outside:** Go back to the pool. Walk towards the large glass doors to the pier.
    *   **Door Code:** Use the keypad near the door. Enter **0968**.
    *   (Kate wears mask).
4.  **The Pier:**
    *   Walk all the way to the gazebo.
    *   Talk to **Helena Romanski**. She wants her voice back.
    *   Call James (ring the bell on the gazebo).

### The Blue Helena
5.  **Preparation:**
    *   Go back to the hotel bar.
    *   **Ingredients:**
        *   Cupboard under bar: **Lemon** and **Honey** (crystalized).
        *   **Melting Honey:** Go to the spa bath nearby. Turn the wheel to heat the water. Place **Honey Jar** in water. Retrieve **Liquid Honey**.
        *   **Vodka:** Pick up **Vodka** bottle from the shelf.
6.  **Mixing:**
    *   Look at the mixing machine.
    *   **Setup:** Place **Vodka**, **Lemon**, **Liquid Honey** in their spots.
    *   **Sequence:**
        *   Turn on (I/O).
        *   Press **Vodka** key (2nd from left).
        *   Press **Blue Curacao** key (3rd from left).
        *   Press **Honey** button.
        *   Press **Lemon** button.
        *   Press **Ice** button.
        *   Press **Shake/Serve** (Rightmost).
    *   You get the **Blue Helena**.

### Leaving
7.  **The Concert:**
    *   The cocktail sends Helena into song. She breaks the glass.
    *   She agrees to leave. You return to the Airship.

## Final Return & Ending

### Escape from Komkolzgrad
1.  **Preparation:**
    *   You land back in Komkolzgrad.
    *   **Cage:** Helena sings to distract Serguei.
    *   Walk to the cage. Use **Metal Shears** on the lock.
2.  **Recovering Hands:**
    *   While Serguei is distracted/trapped or after you free Helena, run up to Serguei's control room (Smelting room).
    *   Pick up **Oscar's Hands** from where Serguei left them (or from Serguei if he is incapacitated).
    *   Run to the elevator with Helena.
3.  **The Train (Final):**
    *   Run back to the train.
    *   **Dynamite:** (If you didn't blow up the Giant leg earlier, do it now to stop pursuit).
    *   **Oscar:** Give **Oscar's Hands** to Oscar.
    *   Depart.

### Final Journey
1.  **Snow:**
    *   The train stops in a snowy field.
    *   Walk to the front of the train.
    *   Witness the meeting with **Hans Voralberg**.
    *   **Ending:** Kate makes her choice to join the adventure.
    *   **Congratulations! You have completed Syberia.**
