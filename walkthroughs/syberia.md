## General Tips

*   **Controls:** Left-click to move and interact. Double-left-click to run. Right-click to open the inventory.
*   **Inventory:** Items are stored in the inventory. You must select an item to "equip" it, then click on the object in the world you want to use it on.
*   **Documents:** Always read documents (click the eye icon in inventory). They often contain vital phone numbers or clues.
*   **Dialogue:** Exhaust all dialogue options. New topics appear as you learn information.
*   **Phone:** You can use Kate's cell phone to call contacts. Dial numbers manually or select from the directory.

## Part 1: Valadil√®ne

### The Hotel & Arrival
1.  **Lobby:** 
    *   Pick up the **Advertising Brochure** from the notice board on the wall to your left.
    *   Try to take the gears from the table where the boy (Momo) is sitting. He won't let you.
    *   Walk to the reception desk. Try to talk to the innkeeper; he ignores you.
    *   Pick up the **Reception Bell Key** on the corner of the desk.
    *   **Puzzle (Bell):** Use the **Reception Bell Key** on the small bell automaton holding a hammer. Push the red button. The bell rings.
    *   Talk to the innkeeper. Select "Help". He shows you to your room.
2.  **Room #6:**
    *   Open the drawer of the nightstand. Pick up **Fax #1** (The Letter from Boss).
    *   Open your inventory, select the Cell Phone. Dial **12-458-902** (or select "Office") to call your boss, Mr. Marson.
    *   Go downstairs.
3.  **Lobby (Again):**
    *   Talk to the innkeeper again. Select "Mission". He gives you **Fax #2**.
    *   Go to the table where Momo was sitting (he is gone now).
    *   Pick up **2 Cogs** from the table.
    *   Pick up **2 Cogs** from the floor.
    *   Exit the hotel.

### The Notary
1.  **Main Street:**
    *   Walk to the left (down screen). Click near the bottom of the screen to proceed toward the Notary's office.
    *   Pass the bakery. Continue until you reach a house with an automaton gatekeeper.
    *   Pick up the **Valadil√®ne Gazette** from the bench on the left. (Read it in inventory for backstory on Anna Voralberg).
2.  **Notary's Door:**
    *   Click on the automaton at the door to zoom in.
    *   **Puzzle (Door):**
        1.  Place **Fax #2** (Notification of Settlement) in the automaton's hand.
        2.  Pull the **middle lever** on the automaton's chest.
        3.  Pull the **right lever** (down by its hip).
    *   The door opens. Enter.
3.  **Notary's Office:**
    *   Enter the office door on the left.
    *   Sit in the chair. Talk to the Notary.
    *   Select "Mission". He gives you the **Deed of Sale**.
    *   Talk about other topics. He gives you **Anna's Letter**.
    *   Exit the office.
4.  **Hallway:**
    *   Before leaving the building, look at the coat rack in the waiting area.
    *   Pick up the **Telescopic Key**.
    *   Leave the building.

### The Voralberg Factory
1.  **Factory Gate:**
    *   Walk right, past the hotel, all the way to the other end of town.
    *   Approach the large gates.
    *   Zoom in on the lock mechanism on the small pedestrian door.
    *   **Puzzle (Gate):**
        1.  Use the **Telescopic Key** on the top keyhole (held by the automaton).
        2.  Turn the key to wind the spring.
        3.  Pull the lever at the bottom.
    *   Enter the factory grounds.
2.  **Fountain & Paths:**
    *   You are at a central fountain intersection.
    *   **Path 1 (North/Upper Right):** leads to the House and Station.
    *   **Path 2 (West/Left):** leads to the Factory floor.
    *   Take the path to the **House** (North/Upper Right).
3.  **Voralberg House:**
    *   Walk past the front door (it's locked). Go around the RIGHT side of the house.
    *   Continue into the back gardens.
    *   Go to the far back of the garden (navigate through the hedge maze) to find a wall fountain.
    *   **Puzzle (Fountain):**
        1.  Zoom in on the basin.
        2.  Use the **Telescopic Key** on the lock.
        3.  The mechanism opens. Pick up the **Voralberg Key**.
    *   Return to the back of the house.
4.  **The Attic:**
    *   Locate the tall ladder mechanism at the back of the house.
    *   Use the **Voralberg Key** on or near the ladder instructions plate. The ladder descends.
    *   Climb up.
    *   Walk left to the desk. Open the roll-top.
    *   Pick up **Anna's Diary** and the **Ink Bottle**.
    *   Read the diary to learn about Hans.
    *   Walk to the right side (dark corner). Click the lightbulb to turn on any light.
    *   Momo appears. Talk to him. Ask about "Mission", "Hans". He gives you **Pencil and Paper**.
    *   **Puzzle (Mammoth):**
        1.  Look at the wooden beam where the light is shining. There is a mammoth drawing.
        2.  Use **Pencil and Paper** on the drawing to get the **Pencil Tracing of Mammoth**.
    *   Give the **Tracing** to Momo. He gets excited and runs off.
    *   Follow Momo. Climb out the window/ladder.

### The Secret Cave
1.  **The Park:**
    *   Go back to the central fountain. Walk left (West) toward the factory, but stay outside.
    *   Actually, follow Momo OUT of the factory grounds entirely, back to the village street.
    *   Go slightly left from the factory gate to the Park entrance (an open gate with steps going down).
    *   Follow Momo up the long stairs to the Dam.
2.  **The Dam:**
    *   Momo is sitting on a rock. Talk to him.
    *   Try the wheel on the dam; it's stuck.
    *   Walk past Momo to the boat. Try to get the **Oar**; it's drifting.
    *   Pick up the **Broken Lever** lying on the ground near the dam.
    *   Go back to the boat. Use the **Broken Lever** on the **Oar** to drag it closer. Pick up the **Oar**.
    *   Talk to Momo. Use the "Help" option (or "Help" + Oar).
    *   Kate uses the Oar as a lever, Momo pushes. The dam opens.
3.  **The Cave:**
    *   Go back down the stairs to where the river was. It is now shallow.
    *   Cross the river bed and enter the cave on the far side.
    *   Walk to the end. Pick up the **Mammoth Doll** from the ground.
    *   Return to the Factory grounds.

### The Factory Floor & Construction
1.  **Warehouse:**
    *   From the central fountain, take the Left path into the large building.
    *   Inside, activate the forklift (flip the switch). It moves a cylinder.
2.  **Assembly Automaton:**
    *   Enter the door behind where the forklift was (inner workshop).
    *   You see a hanging automaton frame.
    *   Pull the winch handle. It lowers but is unfinished.
    *   Exit back to the main floor.
3.  **Power Room:**
    *   Walk to the far back right of the factory floor.
    *   Enter the room with the water wheel mechanism.
    *   Walk behind the mechanism. Pull the large chain/lever.
    *   The water wheel engages. The factory has power.
4.  **Anna's Office (Factory):**
    *   In the main factory hall, climb the metal stairs to the office (catwalk).
    *   Inside, look at the desk. Pick up:
        *   **Letters to Hans**
        *   **Factory Blueprints**
        *   **Train Blueprints**
        *   **Valadil√®ne Voice Cylinder**
        *   **Toy Man** (Hans & Anna statuette)
    *   Look at the bookshelf. Pull the white book. A secret cabinet rotates.
    *   Pick up the **Music Cylinder**.
5.  **Manufacturing Legs:**
    *   Go downstairs to the main floor. Find the control console near the conveyor belt.
    *   **Puzzle (Console):**
        1.  Insert **Factory Blueprint** into the slot.
        2.  Turn the dial/wheel to select the **Legs** (bottom icon light).
        3.  Flip the switch on the left to select the material (Left/Red-ish setting is correct for wood).
        4.  Press the right button to start production.
    *   Wait for the machine to finish.
    *   Walk to the end of the belt. Pick up the **Wooden Legs**.

### The Train Station
1.  **Assembly:**
    *   Go back to the inner workshop (where the hanging automaton is).
    *   Use the **Wooden Legs** on the automaton.
    *   The automaton (Oscar) wakes up. Talk to him.
    *   He leaves for the station.
2.  **Station:**
    *   Exit the factory building. Go to the Station (North path from fountain).
    *   Enter the train carriage.
    *   Talk to Oscar in the ticket booth (on the platform).
    *   Select "Mission". He needs a ticket.
3.  **Ticket Office:**
    *   Go to the ticket window (on the platform).
    *   Talk to Oscar. He gives you a **Train Ticket** and a **Train Release Permit**.
4.  **Legal Approval:**
    *   You need the permit stamped.
    *   Run all the way back to the **Notary's Office** in town.
    *   Go to the reception desk (Notary is gone).
    *   **Puzzle (Stamp):**
        1.  Open the head of the stamping automaton on the desk.
        2.  Pour the **Ink Bottle** into the head.
        3.  Place the **Train Release Permit** on the flat pad.
        4.  Push the red button.
    *   Pick up the **Stamped Train Release Permit**.
    *   Return to the Station.
5.  **Items & Depart:**
    *   Board the train.
    *   Give the **Stamped Train Release Permit** to Oscar.
    *   Talk to Oscar. He says he has no "items" for the display.
    *   Go to the middle room of the train (Display Room).
    *   **Puzzle (Shelves):**
        *   Place **Mammoth Doll** on the shelf.
        *   Place **Music Cylinder** on the shelf.
        *   Place **Valadil√®ne Voice Cylinder** on the shelf.
        *   Place **Toy Man** (statuette) on the central pedestal.
    *   Talk to Oscar again. He is satisfied.
    *   Give him the **Train Ticket**.
    *   He says the train must be wound up.
6.  **Winding the Train:**
    *   Exit the train.
    *   Cross the tracks to the far platform.
    *   Walk down the platform to the wheel mechanism.
    *   Turn the wheel, then pull the lever.
    *   Return to the train.
    *   Depart Valadil√®ne.

## Part 2: Barrockstadt

### The University
1.  **Arrival:**
    *   Walk right along the platform. Pick up the **Hook** from the ground.
    *   Walk left, exit the station to the canal side.
    *   Talk to the Station Master. Ask about the "Hook".
    *   Re-enter the station, cross the bridge, and exit to the right (University Entrance).
2.  **Rectors & Library:**
    *   Enter the University hall.
    *   Go to the central doors. Enter the Rectors' office.
    *   Talk to the Rectors. Ask about "Money" (they will pay you to fix the bandstand).
    *   Exit. Go upstairs to the Library.
    *   Climb the ladder. Pick up the **Blue Book** (on Amerzone Cuckoos) which is upside down.
3.  **Professor Pons:**
    *   Go to the other side of the main hall (Skeleton display).
    *   Talk to Professor Pons. Ask about "Hans".
    *   He asks for a Mammoth toy. Give him the **Mammoth Doll**.
    *   He invites you to his lab.
    *   Follow him to the Lab (nearby door).
    *   Pick up the **Barrockstadt Voice Cylinder** from the back counter.
    *   Pick up the **Yangala-Cola Powder** and **Test Tube Holder** from the benches.
    *   Talk to Pons about everything.

### The Secret of Sauvignon
1.  **Station Master:**
    *   Go back to the Station Master. Ask about **Sauvignon**. He acts suspicious.
    *   Follow him back inside the station. Ask again. He admits there is a garden.
    *   He opens the garden gate for you.
2.  **The Garden:**
    *   Go to the far left of the train tracks (Platform end).
    *   Cross the bridge. Enter the garden.
    *   Walk to the very end. Pick up the **Sauvignon Grapes**.
3.  **The Cuckoos:**
    *   Go back to the train.
    *   Find the ladder near the train that leads to the mechanical eagle. Birds are blocking it.
    *   Give the **Sauvignon Grapes** to the birds. They leave.
    *   Climb the ladder.
    *   Use the **Test Tube Holder** on the eagle nest to retrieve the **Cuckoo Egg**.

### The Bandstand
1.  **Fixing the Machine:**
    *   Go outside the University entrance to the courtyard Bandstand.
    *   Zoom in on the door.
    *   Place the **Cuckoo Egg** in the empty scale pan.
    *   Turn the wheel. Enter the bandstand.
    *   Pull the lever inside. The music plays.
2.  **Payment:**
    *   Return to the Rectors.
    *   Ask about "Money". They give you **100 Dollars**.

### The Locks
1.  **The Barge:**
    *   Go to the canal side.
    *   Talk to the couple on the barge. Give them the **100 Dollars**.
    *   They give you a **Lock Key** (pick it up from the ground).
2.  **Lock Control:**
    *   Walk down the canal path to the control panel.
    *   Look at the sign with the phone number: **27-66-67-42**.
    *   Call the number with your cell phone.
    *   **Codes:**
        *   **#4:** Lower water.
        *   **#2:** Open sluice.
        *   **#1:** Close sluice.
    *   **Execution:**
        1.  Use **Lock Key** to open the panel.
        2.  Enter code: **4, 2, \*** (Lowers water, opens gates). The barge enters.
        3.  Enter code: **4, 1, \*** (Raises water, closes gates). The barge exits.
3.  **Departure:**
    *   Walk back to the platform.
    *   Go to the far right end (The Wall).
    *   Talk to Captain Ombu.
    *   Look through the telescope. Adjust the focus (Right button) until you see the "Cossack" is just a tree.
    *   Tell the Captain. He lets you pass.
    *   Board the train. Oscar says it needs winding.
    *   Exit train. Wind the train (using the mechanism the barge pulled over).
    *   Re-board and depart.

## Part 3: Komkolzgrad

### The Industrial Complex
1.  **The Giant:**
    *   Train stops. Oscar says his hands are jammed.
    *   Exit train. Walk forward to the giant automaton worker.
    *   Climb the ladder into the cockpit.
    *   Pick up the **Komkolzgrad Voice Cylinder** and the **Handle** from the shelf.
    *   Insert **Handle** into the control slot.
    *   Push Handle **UP** once. The giant moves.
    *   Push Handle **UP** again. The giant is over the train.
    *   Press the **Red Button**. The giant winds the train.
    *   Pull Handle **DOWN** twice. The giant returns.
    *   Exit cockpit.
2.  **The Thief:**
    *   You see a man running from the train.
    *   Go into the train. Oscar is tied up and his hands are gone!
    *   Go back to the Giant's cockpit.
    *   Pick up the **Metal Shears** from the shelf (where the items were).
3.  **Infiltration:**
    *   Walk along the platform to the metal wall/window.
    *   Use **Metal Shears** on the metal plate. Enter.

### The Factory
1.  **Boris & The Organ:**
    *   You are in a room with a huge organ.
    *   Pick up the **Screwdriver** from the robot near the organist.
    *   Talk to the organist (Boris). He is drunk.
    *   Go to the back room (Space Capsule area).
    *   Talk to Boris again (he moved).
2.  **Sobering Up Boris:**
    *   Climb the ladder to the crane control.
    *   Pick up the **Key** from the console. Insert it.
    *   **Puzzle (Crane):**
        1.  Move Left.
        2.  Move Down.
        3.  Turn the shower valve (on left).
    *   Boris gets soaked and wakes up.
3.  **Airship Key:**
    *   Talk to Boris. Ask about "Airship". He says the key is lost.
    *   Boris enters the capsule.
    *   Use the **Key** (Boris's Key) on the capsule console. Open the panel.
    *   **Launch:**
        1.  Connect the wires.
        2.  Turn on the power.
        3.  Press the launch button.
    *   It fails. Boris says he needs **Vodka**.
    *   He throws out the **Crank Handle**. Pick it up.
4.  **Eagle & Vodka:**
    *   Go back to the Organ room.
    *   Go to the scaffolding (Bird Cage).
    *   Use **Crank Handle** on the cage lock. The eagle escapes.
    *   Go to where the eagle landed (near the metal plate entrance).
    *   Pick up the **Vodka** (hidden in the rubble/red water area where the eagle is).
    *   Also pick up the **Airship Key** (The eagle dropped/exposed it).
    *   Return to Boris. Give him the **Vodka**.
    *   Launch him again. He leaves.

### Escape
1.  **The Airship:**
    *   Go up the stairs to the large airship platform.
    *   Try to enter. Locked.
    *   Use **Airship Key**. Enter.
    *   Pull the lever. It doesn't work.
    *   Go back to the control room (Smelting controls).
    *   Serguei is there. He has the hands. He locks you in.
    *   Talk to Serguei via the monitor. He wants Helena Romanski.
    *   You must find her.
2.  **The Museum:**
    *   Go back to the Organ room area. Locate the elevator (now active/accessible or previously missed).
    *   Enter the Museum/Director's office.
    *   Open the drawer. Get the **Scrapbook**.
    *   Use your cell phone. Call the number in the scrapbook (Helena's agent).
    *   You learn she is in Aralbad.
3.  **Monorail:**
    *   Go to the monorail station in the complex.
    *   You need to activate it.
    *   (Specifics: This usually transitions automatically or requires simple navigation to the rail head once the plot triggers).
    *   Actually, you use the Airship to go to Aralbad. Wait... Serguei stops you.
    *   Ah, you need to use the **Space Capsule** track or call the cosmonaut?
    *   Correction: You take the Airship *after* getting the key, but it takes you to Aralbad?
    *   **Correction:** You fly the Airship to Aralbad to GET Helena.
    *   Pull the lever in the Airship again. Now it works (or you simply leave).

## Part 4: Aralbad

### The Hotel
1.  **Arrival:**
    *   Exit the Airship. Enter the hotel lobby.
    *   Talk to Felix the manager. He refuses entry.
    *   Go to the utility closet (small door to the left). Pick up **Detergent**.
    *   Go outside. Pour **Detergent** into the fountain.
    *   Go inside. Ring the bell on the desk. Felix leaves to check the foam.
2.  **Infiltration:**
    *   Go behind the desk.
    *   Read the **Guest Register**. Helena is in the Presidential Suite. Note the code: **1270**.
    *   Pick up the **Brochure** (Blue Helena recipe).
    *   Push the **Red Button** to unlock the pool gate.
3.  **The Pool:**
    *   Enter the pool area.
    *   Automaton James blocks the way.
    *   Go to the code panel. Enter **1270**. The door opens.
    *   Enter the locker room.
    *   Pick up the **Gas Mask** (hanging).
    *   Look for a **Temporary Access Card** on the floor. Code: **0968**.
4.  **The Pier:**
    *   Go back to the pool. Walk toward the sea doors.
    *   Use the keypad. Enter **0968**.
    *   Kate wears the mask. Go outside.
    *   Meet **Helena Romanski** at the gazebo. Talk to her.
    *   She has lost her voice. She needs a "Blue Helena" cocktail.
    *   Call James (ring the bell nearby).

### The Blue Helena
1.  **Preparation:**
    *   Go to the hotel bar.
    *   **Ingredients:**
        *   Take **Vodka** from the shelf.
        *   Take **Lemon** and **Honey** (crystallized) from the cupboard.
    *   **Liquifying Honey:**
        *   Go to the the hot tub/spa area inside.
        *   Turn the wheel to heat the water.
        *   Put the **Jar of Honey** in the water. Get **Liquid Honey**.
2.  **Mixing:**
    *   Go to the bar mixing machine.
    *   Place **Vodka**, **Lemon**, and **Liquid Honey** in their slots.
    *   Look at the music sheet on the counter:
        *   **Vodka:** Bass clef (Button 2).
        *   **Blue Curacao:** Treble clef (Button 3).
        *   **Honey:** Button 4.
        *   **Lime:** Button 5.
        *   **Ice:** Snowflake button.
        *   **Shake:** Waiter button.
    *   **The Recipe:**
        1.  Switch to I (On).
        2.  Press **Vodka** (2nd key).
        3.  Press **Blue Curacao** (3rd key).
        4.  Press **Honey** (Honeycomb icon).
        5.  Press **Lemon** (Lemon icon).
        6.  Press **Ice** (Snowflake).
        7.  Press **Shake** (Man).
    *   Helena drinks it and sings. She breaks the glass.
    *   Departure to Komkolzgrad.

## Part 5: Return to Komkolzgrad

### Escape
1.  **The Cage:**
    *   You land. Helena sings to distract Serguei.
    *   Use **Metal Shears** on the padlock of the cage you are in.
2.  **Recovering Hands:**
    *   Run to the organ room.
    *   Go to the smelting control room (where Serguei was).
    *   Pick up **Oscar's Hands** from the desk (or Serguei's body area if event triggered).
3.  **Underground:**
    *   Return to Helena. Take the elevator down to the mine/train level.
    *   Run to the train.
    *   **Explosives:**
        *   Wait, the Giant blocks the way?
        *   Go to the crate in the mine near the elevator. Pick up **Dynamite**.
        *   Place **Dynamite** on the Giant Automaton's leg.
        *   Run!
    *   The giant collapses, forming a bridge.
    *   Cross to the train.
    *   Give **Oscar's Hands** to Oscar.
    *   Depart.
