**Game:** Syberia (2002)  
**Genre:** Point-and-Click Adventure  
**Estimated Completion Time:** 8-12 hours  
**Difficulty:** Medium  
**Missable Content:** None (story-driven, linear progression)

## Getting Started

### Controls & Interface

**Mouse Controls:**
- **Gold Ring Cursor** - Move to location
- **Hand Cursor** - Pick up item
- **Magnifying Glass** - Examine closer
- **Dialogue Bubble** - Talk to character
- **Half Magnifying Glass** - Use/interact with item

**Keyboard:**
- **ESC** or **Right Mouse Button** - Open inventory
- **Space** - Skip dialogue (hold)
- **F5** - Quick save
- **F9** - Quick load

### Important Tips

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Save Frequently!** The game does not autosave. Use F5 to quick save regularly, especially before attempting complex puzzles.

- **Explore thoroughly** - Click on everything to gather information
- **Read all documents** - They contain important clues
- **Talk to everyone** - Exhaust all dialogue options
- **Use your phone** - Check it regularly for important calls
- **Take your time** - Syberia rewards patience and observation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I miss anything important?
**A:** No, Syberia is a linear story-driven game. You cannot miss critical items or progress. The game won't let you leave an area until you've completed all necessary tasks.

### Q: How do I know what to do next?
**A:** Talk to all characters, examine all objects, and read your documents. Oscar and other NPCs will often provide hints about your next objective.

### Q: I'm stuck on a puzzle. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Re-examine the area for visual clues
2. Review documents in your inventory
3. Talk to nearby characters again
4. Try combining items in your inventory
5. Look for interactive objects you might have missed

### Q: How long does the game take to complete?
**A:** First-time players typically complete Syberia in 8-12 hours, depending on puzzle-solving speed and exploration thoroughness.

### Q: Should I play with a walkthrough?
**A:** Syberia is best experienced without a guide on your first playthrough. The puzzles are logical and fair. Only consult a walkthrough if you're truly stuck for an extended period.

### Q: Is there a sequel?
**A:** Yes! Syberia II continues Kate's journey immediately after the first game. There's also Syberia 3 (2017) and Syberia: The World Before (2022).

### Q: Does this version have achievements?
**A:** No, the 2002 Steam version of Syberia does not include achievements. This is purely a story-driven experience.
