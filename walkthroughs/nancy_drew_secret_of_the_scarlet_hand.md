## General Tips

*   **Difficulty Modes:** Choose between **Junior Detective** (more hints, easier puzzles) and **Senior Detective** (harder challenges, fewer hints).
*   **Talk to Everyone:** Talk to Joanna, Henrik, Alejandro, and Taylor repeatedly. If stuck, go do a lap and talk to everyone again. Interaction is key.
*   **Phone Calls:** Call Bess and George for hints. Don't forget to call numbers you find in documents!
*   **To-Do List:** Check Nancy's desk in the lab regularly. It tracks your progress.
*   **Save Often:** Especially before attempting the Temple Maze or major puzzles.
*   **Key Concept - Mayan Numbers:** 
    *   **Shell** = 0
    *   **Dot** = 1
    *   **Bar** = 5
    *   Read numbers bottom to top. 
    *   *Example:* One dot over three bars = (1 * 20) + (15 * 1) = 35.

## Part 1: Museum Orientation

### Nancy's Hotel Room
1.  **Starting Out:**
    *   Exit the room. You can't do much here yet. Head straight to the museum.

### Beech Hill Museum - First Day
1.  **Lobby:**
    *   Talk to the guard at the desk (if present).
    *   Look at the "Coming Soon" sign for the Monolith.
2.  **Meeting Joanna (The Boss):**
    *   Go to **Joanna Riggs' office**.
    *   Talk to her exhaustively about the internship.
    *   Get the **Lab Key**.
3.  **The Lab:**
    *   Go to the Lab (back of the museum). Use **Lab Key** to enter.
    *   **Nancy's Desk:** Read the "To Do" list.
    *   **Henrik:** Talk to Henrik Van Der Hune (the guy in the lab). Ask about everything.
4.  **Meeting Alejandro:**
    *   Go to the Shipping/Receiving room (near the lab).
    *   Talk to Alejandro del Rio. He's... passionate.
5.  **Meeting Taylor:**
    *   You can't meet him in person yet. Call **Taylor Sinclair** from the office phone (number on Rolodex/list).

## Part 2: The Broken Pot & Lab Work

### Assembling the Vase
1.  **The Pieces:**
    *   Find the box of pottery shards on the workbench in the lab.
2.  **Puzzle:**
    *   Assemble the pot. Left-click to pick up, right-click to rotate.
    *   *Strategy:* Match the red painted figures. The rim pieces are distinct.
    *   Once finished, note the glyphs: **King**, **Pacal**, **Rabbit**, **Shield**, **Snake**.

### The Mayan Number Displays
1.  **Exhibit Hall:**
    *   Go to the Number Wheel display in the main hall.
    *   **Puzzle:** Match the Arabic numbers to Mayan glyphs.
    *   *Solution:* 
        *   81 = 4 dots (top), 1 dot (bottom).
        *   Just counting dots (1) and bars (5) usually works for simple numbers.
    *   Take the **Quisling Report** if offered or directed.

## Part 3: The Investigation Begins

### The Theft
1.  **Crime Scene:**
    *   A jade carving of Pacal is stolen!
    *   A **Scarlet Hand** print is found.
    *   Talk to Joanna, Henrik, and Alejandro about the theft.
2.  **The Mexican Consulate:**
    *   Joanna gives you a package/document to deliver.
    *   Exit museum. Go to **Mexican Consulate** on the map.
    *   Talk to the automated robot/system? No, usually you verify the provenance.
    *   *Note:* You might need to make calls to verifying agencies first.

### Sonny's Disk
1.  **Finding the Disk:**
    *   Look around the lab/office area. You find a floppy disk labeled "Sonny".
    *   Insert it into the computer.
2.  **Password:**
    *   The computer asks for a password.
    *   **Clue:** Sonny loved Koko Kringle bars.
    *   **Password:** Enter **KOKO KRINGLE**.
    *   Read the shipment logs. Look for discrepancies.

## Part 4: The Monolith & Temple

### The Monolith Puzzle
*This is one of the game's main objectives.*
1.  **The Goal:**
    *   Unlock the giant stone monolith in the museum lobby.
    *   You need two things: The **Correct Date** and the **Jade Key**.
2.  **Setting the Date:**
    *   Research indicates the date of King Pacal's ascent is crucial.
    *   **The Date:** **6 Lamat**.
    *   **Dial Puzzle:**
        *   Set the **Inner Dial** (Numbers) to **6** (One bar, one dot).
        *   Set the **Outer Dial** (Glyphs) to the **Lamat** symbol (looks like a star/cross).
3.  **The Jade Key:**
    *   You need to find **6 Jade Pieces** hidden throughout the game to assemble the key.
    *   *Piece 1:* In the display case (you might need a key from Joanna).
    *   *Piece 2:* Hidden in the Podcast/Audio exhibit (use Headphones).
    *   *Piece 3:* Given by Poppy Dada (after a phone call/favor).
    *   *Piece 4:* Found in the Lab (check Henrik's area after he.... leaves).
    *   *Piece 5:* In the Temple (Requires solving the maze).
    *   *Piece 6:* Alejandro might have one, or check the shipping crate.
    *   **Assembly:** Combine them in your inventory to form the key.
    *   **Unlock:** Insert the complete Jade Key into the Monolith after setting the date.
    
### The Temple Levels
1.  **Level 1 - Quiz:**
    *   Answer questions about Mayan gods.
    *   *Answers:* Chaac (Rain), Kukulcan (Feathered Serpent/Wind), etc. Consult the museum plaques!
2.  **Level 2 - The Maze:**
    *   Use the computer terminal to log in.
    *   **Username:** `sjoon`
    *   **Password:** `spacebaby`
    *   **The Maze:** Navigate the maze to find a Jade Piece. Use the 'M' key for the map!

## Part 5: Digital Evidence & Radio Puzzles

### The HAM Radio
1.  **Location:**
    *   Found in the Shipping Room (or Henrik's workspace?).
2.  **Frequency 1:**
    *   Tune to **2050**.
    *   Listen to the message.
3.  **Frequency 2:**
    *   Later in the game, find a note with a formula or new frequency.
    *   Tune to **2731**.
    *   You hear Morse code.
4.  **Decryption:**
    *    The Morse code spells out a Nahuatl word.
    *   **Solution:** **COATL** (Snake).

### Henrik's Secrets
1.  **ZIP Disk:**
    *   After a dramatic event (Henrik's "accident"), search his stuff.
    *   Find the **ZIP Disk**.
    *   Read his private notes. He was tracking the smugglers!

## Part 6: Uncovering the Scarlet Hand

1.  **The Suspects Narrow Down:**
    *   Review the shipping logs (Sonny's disk).
    *   Review Henrik's notes (ZIP disk).
    *   Someone is forging provenance documents.
2.  **The Final Key:**
    *   You need one final piece of evidence.
    *   Check the **Poppy Dada** painting again? Or call **Prudence Rutherford** about the "Scarlet Hand" painting.
    *   The "Scarlet Hand" is a fake!

---

## Part 7: The Final Confrontation

1.  **Trap:**
    *   Nancy gets trapped in the temple/exhibit!
    *   The villain reveals themselves.
2.  **Escape Puzzle:**
    *   You are locked in a room. Air is running out (or equivalent pressure).
    *   **Wall Puzzle:** You must create the correct Mayan date/glyph sequence to open the door.
    *   Usually involves the **Pacal** glyphs you studied earlier.
    *   *Solution:* Use the pattern from the Broken Pot: **Shield, King, Snake, etc.** (Verify specific order from game notes).
3.  **Capture:**
    *   Escape the room, and the police (or security) arrive to apprehend the smuggler.
    *   Congratulations, Detective!
