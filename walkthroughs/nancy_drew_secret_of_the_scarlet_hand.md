## General Tips

*   **Difficulty Modes:** Junior Detective vs. Senior Detective.
*   **Talk to Everyone:** Joanna, Henrik, Alejandro, and Taylor. Re-visit them often.
*   **Phone Calls:** Call Bess, George, and any numbers you find.
*   **To-Do List:** Check Nancy's desk in the lab regularly.
*   **Save Often:** Especially before puzzles and the end-game.

## Part 1: Museum Orientation

### Nancy's Hotel Room
- [ ] Exit the room and head to the museum.

### Beech Hill Museum - First Day
- [ ] Talk to the guard at the desk.
- [ ] Look at the "Coming Soon" sign for the Monolith.
- [ ] Go to **Joanna Riggs' office**.
- [ ] Talk to Joanna about the internship.
- [ ] **Collect Item:** Get the **Lab Key** from Joanna.
- [ ] Go to the Lab (back of the museum) and use the **Lab Key** to enter.
- [ ] Read the "To Do" list on Nancy's Desk.
- [ ] Talk to **Henrik Van Der Hune** in the lab about everything.
- [ ] Go to the Shipping/Receiving room.
- [ ] Talk to **Alejandro del Rio**.
- [ ] Use the office phone to call **Taylor Sinclair** (number on Rolodex).

## Part 2: The Broken Pot & Lab Work

### Assembling the Vase
- [ ] Find the box of pottery shards on the workbench in the lab.
- [ ] **Puzzle:** Assemble the pot (Match red painted figures).
- [ ] Note the glyphs: **King**, **Pacal**, **Rabbit**, **Shield**, **Snake**.

### The Mayan Number Displays
- [ ] Go to the Number Wheel display in the main hall.
- [ ] **Puzzle:** Match Arabic numbers to Mayan glyphs.
    - *Hint:* 81 = 4 dots (top), 1 dot (bottom).
- [ ] Take the **Quisling Report** if offered.

## Part 3: The Investigation Begins

### The Theft
- [ ] Discover the theft of the Pacal carving and the **Scarlet Hand** print.
- [ ] Talk to **Joanna** about the theft.
- [ ] Talk to **Henrik** about the theft.
- [ ] Talk to **Alejandro** about the theft.
- [ ] Receive package/document from Joanna for the consulate.
- [ ] Exit museum and go to the **Mexican Consulate**.
- [ ] Talk to the system/robot regarding provenance validation.

### Sonny's Disk
- [ ] Find the floppy disk labeled "Sonny" in the lab/office area.
- [ ] Insert the disk into the computer.
- [ ] **Puzzle:** Enter Password.
    - *Clue:* Koko Kringle bars.
    - *Solution:* **KOKO KRINGLE**
- [ ] Read shipment logs and note discrepancies.

## Part 4: The Monolith & Temple

### The Monolith Puzzle
- [ ] **Goal:** Unlock the stone monolith in the lobby using the **Correct Date** and **Jade Key**.
- [ ] **Set the Date:**
    - [ ] Set Inner Dial (Numbers) to **6** (One bar, one dot).
    - [ ] Set Outer Dial (Glyphs) to **Lamat** (Star/Cross symbol).
- [ ] **Collect Jade Pieces:**
    - [ ] Piece 1: Display case (may need key).
    - [ ] Piece 2: Podcast/Audio exhibit (Use Headphones).
    - [ ] Piece 3: From **Poppy Dada** (call/favor required).
    - [ ] Piece 4: In the Lab (Henrik's area).
    - [ ] Piece 5: In the Temple Maze.
    - [ ] Piece 6: Alejandro / Shipping crate.
- [ ] Combine all 6 pieces in inventory to make the **Jade Key**.
- [ ] Insert **Jade Key** into the Monolith (after setting the date).

### The Temple Levels
- [ ] **Level 1 Quiz:** Answer questions about Mayan gods (Chaac, Kukulcan, etc.).
- [ ] **Level 2 Maze:**
    - [ ] Log in to computer terminal.
        - *Username:* `sjoon`
        - *Password:* `spacebaby`
    - [ ] Navigate the maze (Use 'M' for map) to find the Jade Piece.

## Part 5: Digital Evidence & Radio Puzzles

### The HAM Radio
- [ ] Locate HAM Radio in Shipping Room.
- [ ] **Frequency 1:** Tune to **2050** and listen.
- [ ] **Frequency 2:** Tune to **2731** (found on note).
- [ ] Listen to Morse code.
- [ ] **Puzzle:** Decrypt Morse code.
    - *Solution:* **COATL** (Snake).

### Henrik's Secrets
- [ ] Wait for Henrik's "accident".
- [ ] Search Henrik's workspace.
- [ ] **Collect Item:** Find the **ZIP Disk**.
- [ ] Read private notes on the disk (smuggling tracking).

### The Suspects Narrow Down

- [ ] Review Henrik's notes on ZIP disk.

## Part 7: The Final Confrontation

### Trap & Escape
- [ ] Get trapped in the temple/exhibit by the villain.
- [ ] **Puzzle:** Escape the room before air runs out.
