## 🏆 Tier 1: ABSOLUTE MUST-PLAY
### *Legendary Experiences That Match Your Soul*

### 🐉 The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion
**Platform:** PC (Steam/GOG) | **Expected Rating:** ★★★★★

> *The definitive open-world fantasy. A world so vast and alive, it set the standard for the next decade.*

**Why This is Essential for You:**
- **Unparalleled Freedom:** Like *San Andreas* gave you a state, this gives you a fantasy continent. Go anywhere, do anything.
- **The Dark Brotherhood:** You appreciate stealth (*Splinter Cell*) and atmosphere (*Manhunt*). The assassin guild questline in this game is legendary—arguably the best side-story in RPG history.
- **Living World:** Every NPC has a schedule, a home, and a life. It matches your love for immersive worlds.
- **Prediction: 5 stars - A world you will lose hundreds of hours in.**

**What to Expect:**
- **Genre:** Open-World RPG
- **Strengths:** Quest design, exploration, atmosphere, music (Jeremy Soule).

---

### �️ Hitman: Blood Money
**Platform:** Steam (Bought) | **Expected Rating:** ★★★★⯪

> *The perfect stealth sandbox. Where murder is an art form and you are the artist.*

**Why This is Essential for You:**
- **Social Stealth Perfection:** You've enjoyed stealth games, but this is different. You hide in plain sight, wear disguises, and manipulate the AI.
- **Creative Kills:** Swap a prop gun for a real one at an opera rehearsal. Rig a BBQ to explode. Push a target off a balcony. It’s a dark puzzle game masked as a shooter.
- **Atmosphere:** Jesper Kyd's soundtrack creates a mood that is classy, tense, and professional.
- **Prediction: 4.5 stars - The peak of the classic Hitman series.**

**What to Expect:**
- **Genre:** Stealth Action
- **Strengths:** Level design, replayability, creative freedom, atmosphere.
