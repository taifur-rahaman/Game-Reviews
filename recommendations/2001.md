### 🔮 Gothic 1
**Platform:** PC (Steam/GOG) | **Expected Rating:** ★★★★☆

> *• NOT STARTED - A raw, unforgiving RPG in an isolated prison colony.*

**Why This Matches My Preferences:**
- Deep RPG mechanics and world-building (reminds of Mass Effect's narrative density).
- Gritty, dark, atmospheric fantasy setting enclosed inside a magical barrier.
- Complete freedom where factions react to you, and the world is highly interactive and dangerous.
- No hand-holding; you start as a weak prisoner and must earn every bit of power.
- **Prediction: 4-4.5 stars - a cult-classic RPG with unmatched immersion**

**What to Expect:**
- **Genre:** Action RPG, Dark Fantasy
- **Strengths:** Immersive world, faction choices, dark atmosphere, unique progression
- **Note:** Controls are notoriously clunky and have a steep learning curve, but the reward is immense.

### ♟️ Commandos 2: Men of Courage
**Platform:** PC (Steam/GOG) | **Expected Rating:** ★★★★★

> *• NOT STARTED - The pinnacle of World War II real-time tactics.*

**Why This Matches My Preferences:**
- Built by Pyro Studios, this is the blueprint for **Desperados** (which I rated ★★★★☆).
- Control a group of Allied commandos behind enemy lines in highly detailed, open-ended stealth missions.
- Beautiful, high-quality pre-rendered isometric artwork that ages beautifully.
- Demands extreme planning, quicksaves, and coordination of unique character abilities.
- **Prediction: 5-star experience for tactical stealth purists**

**What to Expect:**
- **Genre:** Real-Time Tactics, Stealth, World War II
- **Strengths:** Map details, complex puzzles, variety of tools and strategies
- **Note:** Extremely challenging, even more punishing than Desperados.
